## The Project

Analytics dashboard designed for marketing teams at B2B companies. The main goal was to make large amounts of data immediately readable without sacrificing information density.

## Key challenges

- Managing dashboards with 50+ metrics without visual overload
- Supporting both technical and non-technical users
- Ensuring performance even with large historical datasets

## Results

The redesign led to a 34% increase in task completion rate and a 20% reduction in average time to find key information.
