## Argomenti
1. Past Continuous
2. Past Continuous + Past Simple
3. Connettori: When e While

---

## 1. Past Continuous

**Quando si usa:** azione **in corso** in un momento specifico nel passato.

**Forma:** *was/were* + verbo **-ing**

| Soggetto | Affermativa | Negativa | Domanda |
|----------|-------------|----------|---------|
| I / He/She/It | was sleeping | wasn't sleeping | Was … sleeping? |
| You / We / They | were sleeping | weren't sleeping | Were … sleeping? |

**Esempi:**
- *When I came home, she was sleeping.*
- *What were they drinking?* → *They were drinking beer.*

---

## 2. Past Continuous + Past Simple

Si usano insieme per **raccontare storie**:
- **Past Continuous** = azione di sfondo (in corso)
- **Past Simple** = azione breve che interrompe

*"I was eating when the phone rang."*
→ Stavo mangiando (in corso) quando il telefono squillò (interruzione).

---

## 3. Connettori: When e While

| Connettore | Uso | Esempio |
|------------|-----|---------|
| **when** | Introduce il Past Simple (azione breve) | *I was sleeping **when** the fire started.* |
| **while** | Introduce il Past Continuous (azione in corso) | ***While** I was sunbathing, he arrived.* |

**Due azioni in corso contemporaneamente:**
- *I was watching TV **while** they were playing bingo.*
