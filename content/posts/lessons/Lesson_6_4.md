## 1. Past Simple

**Quando si usa:** azioni accadute e **completate nel passato**.

### Forma regolare → verbo + **-ed**

| Soggetto | Affermativa | Negativa | Domanda |
|----------|-------------|----------|---------|
| I / You / He… | played | didn't play | Did … play? |

> La forma è uguale per tutte le persone. Negativo e domande usano **did/didn't** + infinito.

### Domande

**Chiuse:** *Did + soggetto + infinito?*
- *Did you drink coffee today?* → *Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.*

**Aperte:** *Question word + did + soggetto + infinito?*
- *Where did you go last year?* → *I went to New York.*
- *What did they eat?* → *They ate pancakes.*

**Eccezione – who/what come soggetto:**
- *Who booked your tickets?* → *The travel agent booked them.*
- *What made you happy?* → *Your photo made me happy.*
