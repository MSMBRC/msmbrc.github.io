## Argomenti
1. Ripasso delle 3 forme di futuro
2. Fare piani: suggerimenti e richieste educate

---

## 1. Le 3 forme di futuro – Ripasso

| Forma | Uso | Esempio |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **Present Continuous** | Piani fissi già programmati (in agenda, con orario/luogo/persona) | *"I'm flying to Bermuda on September 5th."* |
| **Going to** | Intenzioni + Previsioni con prove evidenti | *"I'm going to look for a job."* / *"It's going to rain."* |
| **Will (Future Simple)** | Decisioni spontanee, previsioni, frasi di dubbio | *"I'll have a glass of water."* / *"She'll probably be late."* |

### Frasi di dubbio con "will"

*I think I'll… / I will probably… / Maybe he'll… / I hope it will… / I doubt she'll… / Perhaps we'll…*

---

## 2. Fare suggerimenti

| Espressione | Struttura | Esempio |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Shall we…? | + bare infinitive | *Shall we stay another night?* |
| Why don't we…? | + bare infinitive | *Why don't we go to the beach?* |
| Let's… | + bare infinitive | *Let's go by train.* |
| How about…? | + gerundio | *How about going to the store?* |
| What about…? | + gerundio | *What about making some food?* |

---

## 3. Richieste educate

| Espressione | Struttura | Esempio |
|-------------|-----------|---------|
| Could you please…? | + bare infinitive | *Could you please book the hotel?* |
| Can you please…? | + bare infinitive | *Can you please check the times?* |
| Would you mind…? | + gerundio | *Would you mind carrying this?* |
| Do you mind…? | + gerundio | *Do you mind helping me?* |
