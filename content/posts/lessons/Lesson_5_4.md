# Lesson 5.4 – Party Time

## Argomenti
1. Quantifiers: Some / Any
2. Countable e Uncountable Nouns
3. Much e Many
4. A Little e A Few
5. Vocabulary: Cibo e bevande

---

## 1. Some / Any

| Quantifier | Quando si usa | Esempio |
|------------|---------------|---------|
| **some** | Frasi **positive** | *"I'd like some rice."* |
| **any** | Frasi **negative** | *"I don't have any milk."* |
| **any** | **Domande** | *"Do you have any wine?"* |
| **any** | Significato di "qualsiasi" | *"Any type of fruit is fine."* |

---

## 2. Countable vs Uncountable Nouns

| Countable | Uncountable |
|-----------|-------------|
| Hanno il plurale: *apples, oranges* | Non hanno il plurale: *pasta, water* |
| Si contano: *1 orange, 2 oranges* | Si usano con *some*: *some pasta* |

> I liquidi e gli ingredienti molto fini (zucchero, riso) sono sempre uncountable.

**Uncountable irregolari** (plurale in italiano, singolare in inglese):

| Inglese (uncountable) | Italiano |
|-----------------------|----------|
| information | informazioni |
| news | notizie |
| advice | consigli |
| furniture | mobili |
| money | soldi |

---

## 3. Much / Many / A lot of

| Quantifier | Con | Uso |
|------------|-----|-----|
| **many** | Countable | Frasi neg./domande: *"I don't eat many vegetables."* |
| **much** | Uncountable | Frasi neg./domande: *"I don't eat much meat."* |
| **a lot of / lots of** | Entrambi | Frasi positive: *"I eat a lot of fruit."* |
| **how many** | Countable | *"How many hamburgers are there?"* |
| **how much** | Uncountable | *"How much sugar do you want?"* |

---

## 4. A Little / A Few

| Quantifier | Con | Significato |
|------------|-----|-------------|
| **a few** | Countable | pochi: *"I have a few oranges."* |
| **a little** | Uncountable | poco: *"I have a little butter."* |

---

## 5. Espressioni di quantità per Uncountable

- *a piece of* advice / information / news / paper / furniture
- *a loaf of* bread
- *a litre of* water / milk
- *a bottle of* wine / oil
- *a can of* coke
