## Argomenti
1. Past Perfect Simple
2. Ordinare eventi storici
3. Domande al Past Perfect
4. Avverbi con il Past Perfect
5. Quantificatori formali

---

## 1. Past Perfect Simple

**Quando si usa:** per un'azione accaduta **prima** di un altro evento nel passato. Aiuta a mettere in ordine gli eventi.

**Forma:** soggetto + **had** + Past Participle
Forma contratta: *He'd, They'd, I'd…*

| Soggetto | Ausiliare | Negativo |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| I / You / He / We / They | had | hadn't |

*"Many others **had** already **explored** America before Columbus **arrived**."*
→ Prima: avevano esplorato / Dopo: Columbus arrivò

---

## 2. Ordinare eventi

| Primo evento (più lontano) | Secondo evento (più recente) |
|---------------------------|------------------------------|
| Past Perfect (*had + PP*) | Past Simple **o** gerundio |

Esempi:
- *"I had studied English for two months before I moved to London."*
- *"I had worked in a bank for five years before going abroad."*
- *"I arrived late because I had overslept."*

---

## 3. Domande al Past Perfect

**Chiuse:** *Had* + soggetto + Past Participle
- *"Had he studied English in school?"* → *Yes, he had. / No, he hadn't.*
- *"Hadn't you been to the States before?"*

---

## 4. Avverbi con il Past Perfect

| Avverbio | Posizione | Esempio |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| **already** | tra *had* e il PP | *"I'd already registered before getting my VISA."* |
| **previously** | tra *had* e il PP | *"Britain had previously imposed unpopular taxes."* |
| **yet** | fine frase (neg./dom.) | *"She hadn't found her keys yet."* |

---

## 5. Quantificatori formali (per la scrittura storica)

| Espressione | Uso | Esempio |
|-------------|-----|---------|
| a great deal of | grande quantità | *"She had gained a great deal of experience."* |
| a substantial amount of | quantità sostanziale | *"Italy had produced a substantial amount of art."* |
| a considerable amount of | quantità considerevole | *"He had invested a considerable amount of effort."* |
| a number of | un certo numero di | *"Russia had witnessed a number of social reforms."* |
