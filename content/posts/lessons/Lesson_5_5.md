# Lesson 5.5 – Famous Lives

## Argomenti
1. Past Simple del verbo "to be"
2. There was / There were

---

## 1. Past Simple di "to be"

**Quando si usa:** descrizioni al passato, contesto di eventi passati, età, posizione, tempo atmosferico.

### Forme

| Soggetto | Affermativa | Negativa | Domanda |
|----------|-------------|----------|---------|
| I | was | wasn't | Was I…? |
| You | were | weren't | Were you…? |
| He/She/It | was | wasn't | Was he/she/it…? |
| We/They | were | weren't | Were we/they…? |

> I = he/she/it → **was** | You = we = they → **were**

### Risposte brevi

| Domanda | Sì | No |
|---------|----|----|
| Was she American? | Yes, she was. | No, she wasn't. |
| Were they comedians? | Yes, they were. | No, they weren't. |

**Esempi:**
- *It was a warm day on Sunday.*
- *She was 14.* → Aveva 14 anni.
- *I was at school yesterday.*

---

## 2. There was / There were

Passato di *there is / there are*.

| Forma | Uso | Esempio |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **There was** | Singolare | *There was a big party in the square.* |
| **There were** | Plurale | *There were a lot of houses here.* |
| **There wasn't** | Singolare negativo | *There wasn't internet in Ancient Rome.* |
| **There weren't** | Plurale negativo | *There weren't any people at the beach.* |

### Domande e risposte brevi

| Domanda | Sì | No |
|---------|----|----|
| Was there wine in Ancient Rome? | Yes, there was. | No, there wasn't. |
| Were there computers then? | Yes, there were. | No, there weren't. |
